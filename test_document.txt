Sample Company Policy Document

This document outlines our company's customer service policies and procedures.

Key Points:
- All customer inquiries should be responded to within 2 hours
- Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase
- Technical support is available Monday-Friday 9AM-6PM EST
- For billing issues, customers should contact the finance department
- Urgent issues can be escalated to management immediately

Contact Information:
- Customer Service: support@company.com
- Technical Support: tech@company.com
- Billing Questions: billing@company.com
- Management: manager@company.com

Our goal is to provide excellent customer service and resolve all issues quickly and effectively.
